Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page, click on "Forgot Password", and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I update my resume?

Login to your account, navigate to the "My Profile" section, and click "Edit Resume" to upload a new one or make changes.

How do I set up job alerts?

Go to the "Job Alerts" section in your account and select the criteria for jobs you want to receive alerts for.
